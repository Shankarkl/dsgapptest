ABOUT ONE LOYALTY REWARDS

One Loyalty Rewards specialises in the tailoring of insurance broker, underwriter, staff, call centre, customer and corporate assist & lifestyle loyalty programmes; as well as specialise in the creation of mobile apps and debit, credit and gift cards.

At One Loyalty Rewards, we have a wealth of knowledge and use years of experience to design unique and customer-centric membership programmes. We offer a wide range of products and services including Roadside Assist, Home Assist, Medical Assist and Bail Protect, not to mention a range of lifestyle benefits such as discounted print-out and mobi vouchers; a travel desk and travel aggregator; as well as a full VIP concierge desk.

Our philosophy is based upon the knowledge of informed marketing decisions, driven by the intelligent use of acquired data, thereby creating the basis for attracting and retaining the right mix of customers and maximising their lifetime value.

Our delivery is paramount to our ongoing success. We work as an integrated extension of our clients. We value teamwork and know that high performance environments demand it for success.

With a reputation built on solid delivery, we understand the complexities of delivering integrated loyalty marketing solutions, from strategic thinking and systems design to creative execution and operations. We always aspire to exceed our clients' highest expectations and deliver truly world-class solutions and innovative services.
